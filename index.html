My Travel Story

My love for traveling began back when I was 12 years old. My family and I decided to travel to Europe where we visited Spain, France, and Italy for almost 2 months. My favorite part about traveling is learning new cultures, trying new foods, meeting new people, and visiting different tourist attractions. I decided I would create this project in order to plan out my future travel adventures for after I graduate December 2019. I truly look forward to this new chapter in my life and I can't wait to visit and discover different destinations. Below, you can find some pictures of myself while traveling. These places include Georgia, Tennessee, Naples, and New York. I can't wait to keep growing my travel adventures and share with you some places and foods I plan on experiencing while traveling!
